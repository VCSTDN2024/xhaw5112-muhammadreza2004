Detailed information for the 6 week course:

Child minding

Fees: R750.00

Purpose:

To provide basic child and baby care. This entails looking after and taking care of the needs of a child 6 months or younger, 7 months to a years needs, toddler needs and educational toys to entertain and teach them.

Cooking

Fees: R750.00

Purpose:

To prepare and cook nutritious family meals and knowing the nutritional needs and requirements for a healthy body, different types of proteins, carbohydrates and vegetables. This course also teaches you how to plan meals and how to prepare and cook them as well.

Garden maintenance

Fees: R750.00

Purpose:

To provide basic knowledge of watering, pruning and planting in a domestic garden. This means taking care of indigenous and exotic plants, knowing the water limits and restrictions, pruning plants and planting techniques
