Detailed information for the 6 month course:

First Aid:

Total cost: R1500.00

Purpose:

The purpose of the first aid course is to teach you first aid awareness and basic life support content such as treating wounds and bleedings, what to do in case of burns or fractures, how to handle an emergency, teach CPR and what to do when one is in raspatory distress such as choking.

Sewing:

Total cost: R1500.00

Purpose:

The purpose of the sewing course is to teach you how to provide alterations and garment tailoring services. This includes the different type of stiches, threading and using of a sewing machine, sewing buttons, zips, hems and seams, how to alter a piece and designing and sewing new garments.

Landscaping:

Total cost: R1500.00

Purpose:

The purpose of the landscaping course is to provide landscaping services to new and established gardens. This will include working with indigenous and exotic plants and trees, fixing structures such as fountains, statues, benches and much more, how to balance a plant or tree in a garden, aesthetics of plants shapes and colours and garden layout.

Life skills:

Total cost: R1500.00

Purpose:

To provide skills to navigate basic life necessities such as opening a bank account, knowing your rights with the basic labour law, how to read and write as well as basic numeric literacy.
